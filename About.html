About ElegantRide Ethiopia

Making wedding dreams come true, one luxury ride at a time

Our Ethiopian Story

Founded in 2023, ElegantRide Ethiopia began with a single vintage Mercedes and a vision to transform wedding transportation across Ethiopia.

We set out to change that by providing luxury vehicles with impeccable service, ensuring that your arrival and departure are as memorable as the ceremony itself, across all major Ethiopian cities.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional wedding transportation that complements the significance of your special day across Ethiopia.

Our Ethiopian Values

  • Excellence: We maintain our vehicles to the highest standards and train our chauffeurs extensively in Ethiopian hospitality.
  • Reliability: We understand the importance of timing on your wedding day and guarantee punctuality across all Ethiopian cities.
  • Personalization: Every Ethiopian wedding is unique, and we work with you to create a transportation plan that fits your vision.
  • Security: All our vehicles are equipped with GPS tracking for your peace of mind and vehicle security.
